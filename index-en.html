About Us

Since childhood, I accompanied my father in his tourism activities. Inspired by his journey, I started collaborating with travel agencies by offering car rentals for tours. In 2024, my wife and I decided to create our own agency, Soan'Ala Mada Tour. Today, we share our passion for Madagascar through unique and authentic tours.

Our Services

  • Customized tours across Madagascar
  • Car rental with or without driver

Our Destinations

Specialized in the South, South-West, and East regions of Madagascar, we take you to discover natural parks, wild beaches, traditional villages, and the rich culture of the Red Island.
